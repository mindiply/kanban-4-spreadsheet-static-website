Kanban your Spreadsheet by Mindiply
Manage your spreadsheet data on a Kanban board.

How does it work?

1

Import your data

Import an Excel or CSV file containing your data.

2

Choose the data and set up the fields

Select the fields to use as columns and the ones for the cards' labels.

3

Kanban time!

Drag and drop the cards between columns. Switch the field to group the cards by.

4

Save your changes

Export your updated data back to Excel or CSV format.

For a bit more information before jumping in, look at the Help page.
